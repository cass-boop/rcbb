May 5, 2024

River City Big Band presented The Planets and Beyond

The River City Big Band with special guest conductor Allan Gilliland, played to a sold out show at the Yellowbird jazz club. In the concert's first half featured six new works commissioned by River City Big Band from composers Craig Brenan, Tom van Seters, Joel Toews, Stephanie Urquhart, Reid Contreras Woelfle and Simon Williams.  The concert's second half hosted the world premiere of Gustav Holst's "The Planets", reimagined for Big Band by internationally renowned composer and arranger Allan Gilliland.
